Originally established as the Red Cigar Store, Lloyd’s Smoke Shop & Pool Hall has operated at its current location for almost 80 years. The store has expanded over the years from being a full service tobacconist and hub of Wiarton’s pool scene. Pool was so popular at the time that one had to line up for hours outside the store to have a hope of getting to play on one of the three regulation size snooker tables.

Today, we offer a wide array of services including:

  • Four 8-Ball tables;
  • One regulation size snooker table;
  • Helpful advice about playing pool games including: Snooker, Russian Billiards, 8-Ball, and 9-Ball;
  • Homemade ice-cream (seasonal): stop in to see our ever changing selection of flavours;
  • Card and board games: we would love to help you find the perfect game for you whether it is a party game or hard core strategy;
  • Magazines—an extensive collection (over 400 titles)—we have people who drive in from Toronto for magazines they cannot find anywhere else;
  • Books;
  • Cold drinks;
  • Hot Coffee;
  • Snakes;
  • OLG products; and
  • Garbage bag tags for both the Town of South Bruce Peninsula and Georgian Bluffs.
